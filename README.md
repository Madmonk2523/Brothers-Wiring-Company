# Brothers Wiring Concepts Website

## Professional Electrical Contractor Website

This is a clean, professional website for Brothers Wiring Concepts - designed for a local electrical contractor business.

## Adding Your Images and Logo

### 1. Logo
- Place your company logo in `/images/logo.png` or `/images/logo.jpg`
- The logo should be square format (1:1 ratio) for best results
- Recommended size: 200x200 pixels or larger

### 2. Hero Section Image
- Add your main work photo to `/images/hero-electrical-work.jpg`
- This should showcase your best electrical work
- Recommended size: 1200x800 pixels
- Should be high-quality and professional

### 3. About Section Team Photo
- Add a professional photo of you/your team to `/images/team-photo.jpg`
- Recommended size: 800x600 pixels
- Professional appearance recommended

### 4. Service Images
Add service-specific images to `/images/services/`:
- `residential-wiring.jpg` - Residential electrical work
- `commercial-electrical.jpg` - Commercial electrical work
- `panel-upgrades.jpg` - Electrical panel work
- `emergency-repairs.jpg` - Emergency electrical repairs
- `lighting-installation.jpg` - Lighting installations
- `electrical-inspections.jpg` - Electrical inspection work

### 5. Gallery Images
Add project photos to `/images/gallery/`:
- `project-1.jpg` through `project-6.jpg`
- These should showcase your completed electrical projects
- Recommended size: 600x400 pixels each
- High-quality photos of your best work

## Image Guidelines

- **File Formats**: JPG for photos, PNG for logo (if transparency needed)
- **File Size**: Keep images under 500KB each for fast loading
- **Quality**: Use high-resolution, professional-looking images
- **Content**: Show actual electrical work, clean installations, professional appearance

## Customizing Content

### Contact Information
Update the contact details in `index.html`:
- Phone number
- Email address
- Business address
- Service areas

### Services
Modify the services section to match your specific offerings.

### Business Information
Update company name, years of experience, and certifications to match your business.
